# 🐮 Fuzzy Frenzy

A cozy-but-chaotic **mobile match-3 puzzle game** starring farm animals, featuring **original art**, **original music**, satisfying VFX, and a unique interaction system where animals can affect the board in surprising ways.

> **Signature mechanic:** If a **sheep** falls next to a **wolf** while matching, the wolf **eats the sheep** and leaves behind a **breakable bones obstacle**.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

- Classic **match-3** gameplay with level progression
- Animal-themed tiles, obstacles, and special interactions
- Atmospheric level effects like **snow** and **rain** particles

---

## ✨ Features

### 🛒 Shop
An in-game shop for acquiring avatar customization, and boosts to help players progress.

### 💥 Power-Ups
- **Bomb (3×3):** Explodes a 3×3 area on the board.
- **Extra Move:** Grants an additional move to finish a level.
- **Timer Frenzy:** **Freezes time for ~5–10 seconds**, letting the player move freely until time runs out.
- **Black Sheep:** Explodes the row/column slid to.

### 🏆 Achievements
Unlock achievements by completing goals and hitting milestones during play.

### 🥇 Leaderboard (Firebase)
Competitive leaderboard backed by **Firebase**, letting players compare scores and performance.

### 🧑‍🌾 Avatar Customization
Players can personalize their in-game avatar with a customization system that supports selectable cosmetic options (e.g., outfits/accessories). Customization helps players express their style and adds a sense of identity across progression, achievements, and leaderboard play.

### 🎨 Original Art
- **Original visual assets**
- Enhanced presentation with **particles and effects** across different levels

### 🎵 Original Soundtrack
The game features an **original soundtrack** including **two alternating level tunes** (switching between levels), a dedicated **Title/Main Menu theme**, and a high-energy **Timer tune** designed to increase pressure during timed moments.

### 🐺🐑 Animal Interaction System
Dynamic interactions that trigger during matching and board resolution:
- **Wolf + Sheep** adjacency can trigger an “eat” event:
  - Sheep is removed
  - **Bones** are spawned as a **breakable obstacle**
